About

A short introduction to who I am and how I work.

I'm a Graphic Design student at Humber College with a focus on branding, packaging, and editorial design.

My work moves between brand identity, packaging systems, and editorial layout. I'm drawn to the detail that makes a brand feel like itself — a typeface choice, a colour decision, the way a label folds — and I build every project around a clear story, not just a clean surface.

Process

How a project moves from idea to finished design.

i.

Research & Story

Understanding the brand's world before touching colour or type.

ii.

Concept & Sketch

Loose exploration of type, mood, and palette before anything is final.

iii.

Design & Refine

Building the system in Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

iv.

Polish & Present

Tightening detail until the final result feels effortless.
