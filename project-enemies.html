The Brief

What the project set out to do, and the story behind the design.

This poster concept for "Enemies: A Tale for Lads and Lasses" set out to capture narrative tension before a viewer ever sees the trailer. Layered type and a restrained colour palette were used to suggest mood and conflict, letting the composition do the storytelling.
